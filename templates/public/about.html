About KYISA

Kenya Youth Intercounty Sports Association

Who We Are

The Kenya Youth Intercounty Sports Association (KYISA) is the premier organisation dedicated to organising and managing intercounty youth sporting competitions across all 47 counties of Kenya.

Founded with a mission to nurture young athletic talent, KYISA provides a structured platform for youth teams to compete at the highest intercounty level, fostering sportsmanship, teamwork, and excellence.

Through our competitions, we aim to identify, develop, and promote Kenya's future sports stars while building strong community ties across county lines.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To organise and manage competitive intercounty youth sporting events that identify, develop, and celebrate young athletic talent across all 47 counties of Kenya.

🌟

Our Vision

A Kenya where every young athlete, regardless of their county of origin, has the opportunity to develop their sporting potential through fair, competitive, and well-structured sporting programmes.
