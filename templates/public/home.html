What We Do

The Annual Youth Championship
For Ages 18 – 23

KYISA is Kenya's premier annual intercounty sports championship, bringing together talented young athletes aged 18 to 23 from all 47 counties to compete at the highest level.

01

Annual Championship

Every year, KYISA hosts a week-long multi-sport championship where counties field their best youth athletes to compete for glory.

02

Youth Ages 18 – 23

Exclusively for young Kenyans aged 18 to 23 — identifying, developing, and celebrating the next generation of sporting talent.

03

Intercounty Competition

Teams representing all 47 counties go head-to-head in football, basketball, volleyball, handball and more.

04

Results & Stats

Real-time tracking of fixtures, results, standings, and player statistics across all competitions.

Women's Football

High-intensity action on the pitch

KYISA 2025 · Homa Bay

Makueni County Dominates at KYISA Games

Makueni made an emphatic statement at the KYISA games in Homa Bay. The men's football team pulled off a stunning 1-0 victory against defending champions Busia, while the women footballers produced a commanding 2-0 victory over Kajiado to book their semifinal spot.

On the basketball court, the women's team edged Siaya 48-47 in a nail-biting encounter that went down to the wire.

Basketball

Thrilling court action across counties

Champions · 10th Edition

Makueni's Historic Triple Crown

Makueni County recorded their most successful performance at the 10th edition of KYISA Games. The women's football team defended their title for the third consecutive year, defeating Kajiado 5-4 on penalties in a thrilling final.

Makueni also claimed victory in men's basketball, overpowering hosts Homa Bay 78-52. Overall, the county reached five finals — showcasing its emergence as a sporting giant.
